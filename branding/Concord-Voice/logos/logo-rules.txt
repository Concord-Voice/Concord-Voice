Key
main-logo: Primary logo form factor
symbol: Presents just the Moon with Two Stars logo
transparent: Uses a transparent background
inverted: Reverses color scheme to a 'light mode'
hires: High Resolution
vector: Uses vector drawing
noslogan: Removes the slogan "Own Your Voice" from the logo
logo-icon: Is for icon-based use cases
watermark: main-logo rendered at 50% opacity and has a transparent background
animated: Fade in graphics of the main-logo
favicon: used for different icon use cases, including mobile and webapps

Icon Spacing Rules:
Maintain spacing of at least r around the brand icon, where r = 1/3 the unit square around the icon. The unit square is defined by the largest of the icon width or height.
